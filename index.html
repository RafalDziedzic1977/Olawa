Visit Olawa

A great place for sports, cultural activities and relaxation after a busy day.

Top three activities to do at Olawa

Visit Aquapark

Jacob's Thermae, an aquapark built in the center of Olawa, is a great place for sports.

Go for a concert

The European Center is a venue for concerts and other cultural events.

Rest after busy day

The marina on the banks of the Oder River is a great place to relax after a busy day.
